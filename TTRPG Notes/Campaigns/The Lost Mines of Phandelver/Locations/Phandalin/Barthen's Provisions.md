Barthen's is the biggest trading post in [[Phandalin]]. Its shelves stock most ordinary goods and supplies, including backpacks, bedrolls, rope, and rations. The place is open from ==sunup to sundown==. Barthen's ==does not stock weapons or armor==, but characters can purchase other adventuring gear here, with the ==exception== of items that cost more than ==25 gp== Characters in need of weapons or armor are directed to the [[Lionshield Coster]] 

Run by [[Elmar Barthen]]
